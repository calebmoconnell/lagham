1st Lagham Scout Group

1st Lagham (Godstone) Scout Group was founded in 1936 in the village of South Godstone, Surrey, which was known before the arrival of the railways as Lagham. Initially known as 2nd Godstone, the Group's name was changed to assert its independence from Godstone. This is reflected in the Group motto: Nulli Secundus.

This page was set up to catalogue the records and archives of the Group, which cover the scouting County (Surrey) and District (East Surrey), the Group itself and the village of South Godstone.
